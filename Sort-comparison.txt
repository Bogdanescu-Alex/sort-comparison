Sort-comparison

for 100000 elements [0, 999]
quick sort - Average Time over 5 simulations: 0.007200 seconds - faster then merge sort since we are not hitting the worst case scenario where we choose the worst pivot multiple times
merge sort - Average Time over 5 simulations: 0.014200 seconds - faster then insert since O(nlogn) < O(n^2)
insert sort - Average Time over 5 simulations: 2.877600 seconds - faster then selection sort since insert sort is natural
selection sort - Average Time over 5 simulations: 6.826600 seconds
bubble sort - Average Time over 5 simulations: 30.309800 seconds

for 100000 elements [0, 99]
quick sort - Average Time over 5 simulations: 0.062400 seconds
merge sort - Average Time over 5 simulations: 0.024600 seconds
insert sort - Average Time over 5 simulations: 3.470800 seconds
selection sort - Average Time over 5 simulations: 5.855600 seconds
bubble sort - Average Time over 5 simulations: 28.887800 seconds
